    About Me

    Hello!! My name is Brittany Payne and I am from Athens, GA. I am 25 years old, and the oldest of my family. My younger sister is 22 and my brother is 16. Needless to say, they are my children. Over the summer of 2023, I got engaged to my fiance Kellie, and we have 3 dogs and 2 cats. I currently work at a hospital in Athens as a secretary and monitor tech in the Intensive Care Unit.

    After finishing high school, I started driving a school bus while going to Athens Technical College. While there, I graduated with two Associate Degrees: one in Computer Support and the other of Network Specialist. Outside of Athens Tech and Kennesaw State, I have not had any experience with any IT training or certifications.

    One of my main goals is to get back into the education side of Information Technology. I thought my time with kids would end once I left the bus driving, however, I really enjoyed working with kids. After graduating from Kennesaw State, I have thought about going into education to be able to teach Technology at a middle school level. I'm not entirely sure yet, but that is something I have been thinking about.
